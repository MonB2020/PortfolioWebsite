About Me

My name is Monica. I enjoy learning about technology and using it in my everyday life.

I am currently in my second year at BCIT in the Computer Systems Technology program. In the first term, some of the courses included web development, business analysis, business communications, and programming in Java. I particularly enjoyed designing different website layouts. Some courses in the second term included discrete math, computer architecture, and relational database systems. I took an interest in coding in my senior years of high school. This drove me to pursue courses outside my regular curriculum, and even develop personal projects. In my free time, I explore new programming concepts online through Linkedin and Youtube.

On my spare time, I enjoy spending time with my friends and family, baking, reading, spending time outdoors, taking photos, and going for long walks.
